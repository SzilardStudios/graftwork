After buying on Gumroad, you'll receive a download link. Save the file to your computer — it'll be a .xlsx file.

Go to sheets.google.com in your browser. Sign in with any Google account — Gmail counts. It's completely free.

Click File → Import, then drag in the downloaded file. Choose "Replace spreadsheet" and click Import.

Add your company name, logo, or tweak any field to suit your needs. The unlocked version included in your download gives you full flexibility to customise. Prefer us to handle it? Just get in touch and let us know what you need — we're happy to make the changes for you.
